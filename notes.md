* Use 1206 resistors, capacitors
* Use ch32v003 (J4M6) 8 pin controller
    * needs: 100nF capacitor
    * optional: 10kohm resistor
* Use WS2811 LED controllers
    * needs: 100nF capacitor, 100ohm resistor, 33ohm resistor
* Use LEDs of different sizes
* Use SMD pin connectors
* Expose power pads for soldering battery or usb connector.
